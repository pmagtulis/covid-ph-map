Covid-19 in the Philippines

June 11, 2022

The map below shows the state of Covid-19 in the Philippines as of May 29, the period on which granular case data is available.
By region, the dense National Capital Region corners majority of the cases.

Covid-19 in the Philippines

As of May 29, the Southeast Asian country has recorded 3,690,253 Covid-19 cases. Nearly 32% of those cases were detected in Metro Manila or the National Capital Region.


Zoom in and click the map on the right to show regional, provincial and municipal level of data. The darker the color, the higher the cases in the area.


Note that there were over 9,100 cases with no indicated municipal location. There were also some municipalities, which had no available data. This will be updated.


Source: Department of Health
